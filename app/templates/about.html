About MailMockup

Preview, test & perfect your automated emails

See exactly what your system emails look like before they go live – across templates, types, and inboxes.

How it works

Choose an email template, pick an email type (like “Welcome email” or “Password reset”), and instantly see how they look together.

You can also send a test email to any address to preview the final design in your inbox.

  • Select a layout template
  • Pick an email type to preview
  • Send test emails to real inboxes

Purpose

This tool helps companies design and improve their automated emails.

Automated system emails are often inconsistent and hard to visualize. MailMockup solves this by letting users preview each email type with different templates, ensuring a unified brand look.

Test emails can be sent to any address so teams can check real-life rendering before going live.

No more guessing what your automated emails will look like.

For developers & product teams

Developers can modify and create templates at their customer’s request. Before implementation in the software, customers can check and test different templates and approve both design and text.

This simplifies communication between developers and customers by making requirements clearer and facilitating approval through visual previews instead of long email threads.
